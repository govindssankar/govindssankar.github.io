Govind S. Sankar

I am a first-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Duke. I graduated with a Dual Degree (Bachelor + Master of Technology) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I am primarily interested in Algorithms and Complexity. My research so far has been on Approximation and Online Algorithms and Parameterized Complexity.
