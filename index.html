Govind S. Sankar

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Computer Science Department at Duke University. I graduated with a Dual Degree (Bachelor + Master of Technology) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I am broadly interested in algorithms for problems modeling various notions of fairness. I am fortunate to be advised by Kamesh Munagala.
