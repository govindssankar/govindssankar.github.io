Govind S. Sankar

I am a second-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Duke. I graduated with a Dual Degree (Bachelor + Master of Technology) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I am primarily interested in Algorithms and Complexity, primarily Approximation Algorithms. I am advised by Kamesh Munagala .
